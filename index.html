About Us

It all started 12 years ago. A fusion of creativity, talent and skill is what Ordin@trix is all about. Ordin@trix has evolved through the years moving from an interschool festival at the state level to an international event. Over the years we have had students from China, Muscat, Japan and other countries as regular participants.


The event has witnessed gracious presence of celebrities like Mr. Amol Gupte and Miss Manju Singh apart from experts from various technical fields. The movies showcased here have been screened at the movie festivals by PVR Cinemas and CHINH India Festival.

GUIDELINES

  • ONLINE registrations on a first come first served basis will be entertained.
  • The first 20 registrations will be accommodated in each event. Registrations will close on Wednesday, 15 July 2020.
  • Each school must ensure that a student participates in only one event and that they register only one team per event except for Crypt@trix, which invites individual registrations.
  • List of registered schools will be displayed on the Ordin@trix website and on our Facebook page.
  • All future correspondence will be through the email id of the teacher in charge as per the registration form.
  • The timings and links for participation in the final round of events ( wherever applicable as per the event guidelines ) will be shared soon after the registrations.
  • The dates of submission for all the events and the email ids of the event heads are mentioned with the respective event guidelines.
  • Submissions should be made to the respective event heads. Please mention the school name and names of the participants along with the entry.
  • Submissions must not: violate any third party rights, including, but not limited to, copyrights, trademark rights, or rights of privacy and publicity; contain defamatory statements; include threats to any person, place, business, or group; or be obscene or indecent.
  • Please refer to the link on our school website, www.tagoreint.com/vv and/or the Ordin@trix website, ordin.teampyro.tech for the latest updates on the events.
  • Decisions of the organizers & jury members regarding any issue related to Ordin@trix’20.0 will be FINAL AND BINDING on all schools and participants.
  • The final events for Ordin@trix 20.o will be held on Saturday, 25 July 2020 with the announcements of results on TIS, VV school website, the event website and our facebook page on Wednesday, 29 July 2020.
  • To qualify as an OVERALL WINNER , a school must take part in a minimum of 5 events.
  • The students ‘ certificates and Honorary Certificate for the Winning School will be shared in electronic form a week after the declaration of results.
